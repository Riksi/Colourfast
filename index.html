How it works

  1. Click on a colour to select it and click on a tile to paint it but watch out since tiles will fade at random and need to be filled again.
  2. The position of the colours will shuffle each time a colour is selected and the same colour can't be picked twice consecutively.
  3. A tile can't have the same colour as any other tile with which it shares an edge.
